Kayros is an AI-driven technology company dedicated to transforming business operations through intelligent, scalable software solutions. Founded on more than three decades of combined experience in analysis, system design, and application development, Kayros was established with a clear vision: to engineer smarter operational systems that empower organizations to perform at their highest potential.

We specialize in building AI-integrated platforms that enhance communication, streamline workflows, and improve real-time decision-making. Our approach goes beyond traditional software development — we architect structured operational frameworks designed to reduce complexity, increase visibility, and enable sustainable growth.

At Kayros, we believe intelligent technology should not replace human judgment, but strengthen it. That philosophy drives the development of our flagship platform, , an AI-powered patient engagement and operational system built specifically for healthcare organizations.

Our Team

Leadership

Kayros is led by seasoned executives with deep experience in healthcare technology, enterprise systems, and AI-driven operational platforms.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Michael Jimenez-Portal

Michael Jimenez-Portal is a technology executive and entrepreneur with over three decades of experience building and scaling healthcare technology platforms. He is the former CEO and Owner of Assertus Holdings, successfully leading the organization until its acquisition in 2025.

Previously, he served as Senior Vice President of the Health System Division at Evertec, overseeing large-scale healthcare technology initiatives and enterprise system implementations.

Michael and his team were the creators of ProClaim medical billing software, Medicus EHR, and the Assertus Healthcare Clearinghouse — mission-critical platforms supporting healthcare providers nationwide processing over 50 million claims.

He is also Co-Owner of Mis Resultados, the leading electronic clinical laboratory results delivery system, processing millions of lab results every month.

At Kayros, Michael leads the strategic vision to engineer intelligent AI-powered platforms that redefine operational efficiency and structured coordination.

Vice President of Operations

Aileen Iglesias

Aileen Iglesias serves as Vice President of Operations at Kayros, bringing extensive experience in product strategy, operational execution, and AI-focused solution development. With a strong background in product ownership, she specializes in translating complex business and operational requirements into scalable, user-centered technology solutions.

As a product-focused leader, Aileen oversees platform development from concept to deployment — defining product vision, prioritizing features, aligning cross-functional teams, and ensuring that AI-driven solutions deliver measurable impact.

Her expertise ensures that Kayros platforms are not only innovative, but structured, practical, and results-oriented.

Together, Kayros leadership combines proven healthcare technology expertise with advanced AI innovation to engineer the next generation of intelligent operational systems.

Our Approach

Technology Philosophy

At Kayros, technology is not built for automation alone — it is built for intelligent coordination.

We believe artificial intelligence should bring structure to complexity, not replace human judgment. The most effective systems are those that combine AI-driven automation with clear operational frameworks and informed human oversight.

01

Structure Before Scale

Intelligent systems must be architected with disciplined workflows and clear logic before they can scale effectively. We build the foundation first.

02

AI with Purpose

Automation must solve real operational problems, not add unnecessary layers of complexity. Every AI feature we build has a clear, measurable objective.

03

Human-in-the-Loop Design

Technology should enhance decision-making and coordination, ensuring that critical moments remain guided by human insight and judgment.

We design platforms that unify communication, workflow orchestration, and real-time visibility — creating intelligent operational ecosystems that serve both organizations and the people they ultimately support. Because behind every system is a human outcome.
