Kayros Assist is our flagship healthcare platform — built on Kayros' intelligent operational infrastructure. It combines AI automation with live human oversight to deliver structured, scalable, and measurable patient engagement.

The Challenge

Healthcare organizations face increasing operational complexity:

  • High call volumes and fragmented communication channels
  • Administrative overload on clinical and support staff
  • Missed appointments and follow-up gaps
  • Disconnected messaging systems across departments
  • Limited real-time visibility into operational performance

Traditional tools manage tasks. Kayros Assist structures the entire workflow.

The Solution

Kayros Assist integrates AI-powered automation, real-time operational dashboards, and omnichannel communication into one unified platform. It enables healthcare teams to:

  • Automate patient engagement at every touchpoint
  • Prioritize and route communications intelligently
  • Reduce administrative burden on staff
  • Improve coordination between AI and live agents
  • Track cases from initiation to resolution
  • Gain full operational visibility in real time
